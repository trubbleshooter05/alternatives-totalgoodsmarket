FAQs

Is ClickFunnels still worth it?

Yes, for businesses focused heavily on funnel-based selling. But alternatives may offer better value depending on your needs.

What is cheaper than ClickFunnels?

Systeme.io offers a free tier and lower starting price. GoHighLevel and Kartra offer more bundled features.

Which alternative is best for agencies?

GoHighLevel is designed specifically for agencies managing multiple clients.

Can I migrate funnels easily?

Most alternatives allow page rebuilding or importing, though full automation migration may require manual setup.

Which platform has the best automation?

Kartra is often considered strongest for built-in automation workflows.
